Musical Origins

sentiment_very_satisfied

Psychological Ease

Beyond sound, it evolved to mean the ease or relief one feels while enjoying life, hence 'comfort'.

Usage Tip

Use 楽 (Raku) as a noun to refer to an easy life or 楽しい (Tanoshii) to describe a fun activity.
